SECTION 1: WEBSITE NAVIGATION GUIDE
================================================================================

MAIN PAGES AND HOW TO ACCESS THEM:

1. HOME PAGE
   - URL: / (root)
   - Navigation: Click "Home" in the navigation bar
   - Content: Homepage with featured technologies, services overview, impact statistics, and latest news

2. IP PORTFOLIO (Our IP)
   - URL: /ip-portfolio
   - Navigation: Click "Our IP" in the navigation bar
   - Content: Collection of patents, technologies, and innovations available for licensing and commercialization
   - User can search and filter by field and status
   - Includes information about how to license technologies

3. SERVICES
   - URL: /services
   - Navigation: Click "Services" in the navigation bar
   - Content: Core services offered by TPCO including:
     * IP Protection Services
     * Technology Licensing
     * Industry-Academe Matching
     * Startup Incubation
   - Also includes Additional Services and Success Stories

4. RESOURCES
   - URL: /resources
   - Navigation: Click "Resources" in the navigation bar
   - Content: Resources & Tools including:
     * Templates (Legal Templates & Documents)
     * IP 101 Tutorials (Learning Modules)
     * SSF Booking (Shared Service Facilities)
     * Guidelines (Research Guidelines & Best Practices)
   - Events & Workshops section is also accessible here

5. EVENTS & WORKSHOPS
   - URL: /events
   - Navigation: Go to "Resources" page, then click "View All Events" or navigate through the Workshops & Events section
   - Alternatively: Click "Resources" in nav bar, then scroll to "Workshops & Events" section
   - Content: Upcoming and past workshops, seminars, and events
   - Users can register for events directly

6. LATEST NEWS
   - URL: /latest-news
   - Navigation: Click "Latest News" in the navigation bar
   - Content: News articles and updates about TPCO activities

7. ABOUT
   - URL: /about
   - Navigation: Click "About" in the navigation bar
   - Content: Information about USTP TPCO, Mission/Vision/Values, Expert Team, Company Journey/Milestones, Strategic Partners

8. CONTACT
   - URL: /contact
   - Navigation: Click "Contact Us" button in the navigation bar
   - Content: Contact information, office location, phone, email

COMMON NAVIGATION QUESTIONS:

Q: "Where can I find events?" or "Events?" or "Workshops?"
A: To access Events & Workshops, go to the "Resources" page by clicking "Resources" in the navigation bar. Then scroll down to the "Workshops & Events" section or click "View All Events" to see all upcoming and past events. You can also access events directly at /events.

Q: "Where are the services?" or "What services do you offer?"
A: Click "Services" in the navigation bar to view all services offered by TPCO. The Services page includes core services like IP Protection, Technology Licensing, Industry-Academe Matching, and Startup Incubation.

Q: "How do I contact TPCO?" or "Contact information?"
A: Click "Contact Us" button in the navigation bar to access the Contact page. You can also call (088) 856-1738 or email tpco@ustp.edu.ph directly.

Q: "Where can I see patents/technologies?" or "IP Portfolio?"
A: Click "Our IP" in the navigation bar to access the IP Portfolio page. There you can browse, search, and filter patents and technologies available for licensing.

Q: "Where can I find templates/forms?" or "Download documents?"
A: Go to the "Resources" page and click on the "Templates" tab to find legal templates and documents like Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA) and Memorandum of Understanding (MOU).

Q: "Where can I learn about IP?" or "IP tutorials?"
A: Go to the "Resources" page and click on the "IP 101 Tutorials" tab to access learning modules about intellectual property fundamentals.

Q: "How do I book a facility?" or "SSF booking?"
A: Go to the "Resources" page and click on the "SSF Booking" tab to book access to Shared Service Facilities and research equipment.
