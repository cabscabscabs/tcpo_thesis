OVERVIEW
--------
USTP Technology Promotions and Commercialization Office (TPCO) leads technology transfer and innovation in Northern Mindanao through strategic partnerships between academia and industry.

Quick Stats:
- 6 Years of Excellence
- 12 Expert Team Members
- 95% Success Rate
- 24/7 Support Available

OUR MISSION
-----------
To accelerate the translation of USTP research innovations into market-ready technologies that address regional and national development needs while fostering sustainable industry-academe partnerships.

OUR VISION
----------
To be the premier technology transfer office in the Philippines, recognized for excellence in innovation commercialization and as the catalyst for economic development in Northern Mindanao.

OUR VALUES
----------
- Innovation: Fostering creativity and breakthrough thinking
- Integrity: Ethical practices in all partnerships
- Excellence: Commitment to quality and continuous improvement
- Collaboration: Building meaningful partnerships

OUR JOURNEY - KEY MILESTONES
----------------------------
2018 - TPCO Establishment: Founded as USTP's dedicated technology transfer office

2019 - First Patent Grant: Achieved first successful patent registration for USTP innovation

2020 - Industry Partnership Program: Launched formal industry-academe collaboration framework

2021 - CDO b.i.t.e.s. Partnership: Established strategic alliance for startup incubation

2022 - IP Portfolio Milestone: Reached 15 patents granted with active licensing program

2023 - Regional Recognition: Awarded Outstanding Technology Transfer Office in Mindanao

2024 - Innovation Hub Launch: Opened state-of-the-art facility for technology commercialization
